Klopp

Jurgen Klopp spent his entire 12-year playing career at Mainz 05, before going on to become their longest-serving manager from 2001 to 2008, during which time they achieved promotion to the Bundesliga. In 2008, Klopp joined Borussia Dortmund, leading them to back-to-back Bundesliga wins in 2011 and 2012, as well as the DFB-Pokal in 2012, the DFL-Supercup in 2008, 2013 and 2014, and their second appearance in a Champions League final in 2013. Klopp won the German Football Manager of the Year in 2011 and 2012.Jurgen Klopp comes to Liverpool with a big reputation as a manager and due to this the Anfield faithful will be expecting big things from him over the next few years.Klopp comes into Liverpool 8 games into the season after Breden Rogers was sacked due to poor performances.
